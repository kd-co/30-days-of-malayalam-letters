#30DaysOfMalayalamLetters

1st to 31st August 2017

About

Welcome to #30DaysOfMalayalamLetters. This project invites Designers, Illustrators and Graphic Artists to unleash their creative potential by expressing their particular view on Malayalam alphabets. The primary goal is to challenge the design community to recreate entire alphabet from a new perspective.

KD.Co

Kerala Designers Collaborative (or KDCo, as we like to call it) is a collective of Malayali designers, illustrators, graphic artists and other creative professionals. More than a discussion forum, this provides a platform for designers to gain constructive feedback on their work, and be up-to-date with industry standards and trends. #30DaysOfMalayalamLettering is the very first undertaking by KDCo inviting a crowdsourced participation.
